Listr SMS Opt-In

To opt in, text START to the Listr phone number. Listr is a user-initiated conversational messaging service and only sends messages in response to messages initiated by the user. No marketing or promotional messages are sent.

After opting in, users receive a welcome message with instructions. Users can reply STOP to opt out at any time and HELP for assistance. Msg & data rates may apply.
