Listr Terms & Conditions

Listr is a personal SMS shopping list service.

How it works:

  • Text any item (example: eggs) to add it to your list.
  • Text SEND to receive your full list, then your list is cleared.

Message frequency depends on how often you text the service.

Msg & data rates may apply.

Support: Text HELP for help.

Opt-out: Text STOP to unsubscribe.
